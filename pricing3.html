Questions about Chatfuel pricing

How does tiered pricing work on the Pro plan?

Tiered pricing for the Pro plan means that you'll be charged a monthly fee based on the number of users who have interacted with your bot at least once since it was launched. If 51 to 500 users interact with your bot at least once in a given payment period, the fee is $15/month. From 501 to 1,000 users is $20/month, from 1,001 to 2,000 users is $30/month, and from 2,001 to 25,000 is $180/month. If your user base exceeds 25,000 users per month, let’s talk to sales for our Premium plan.

What qualifies as a chat with user?

You will only be charged for users who have interacted with your bot at least once since it was launched. No matter how many subscribers your business has on Facebook or Instagram (100, 20k or 1 million): If your bot had 1,500 open chats with users on Messenger/DMs during the payment period (one month), you will owe $30 for that payment period. Chats that were started in previous payment periods, if not deleted, are still considered open and included in the payment. Check out the tiered pricing list to see how this pricing scales.

What is an adjustment fee?

When you first upgrade to the Pro plan, we'll charge you an estimated fee based on the number of active users who have interacted with your bot at that point. If, during the payment period, your user base exceeds your current tier, you'll also be charged an adjustment fee to make up for the difference. Your rate for the next month will then be based on the new, actual number of open chats with users. For example, say you had 400 active users on the first day of your payment period. Your estimated fee would be $15 for that month. But if, by the end of the month, your user base expanded to 800 users, we will charge a $5 adjustment fee on top of the $15 you already paid. We will then upgrade you to the next tier ($20/month) for the next payment period.

What is your refund policy?

Our goal is to offer the best and the most intuitive conversational marketing platform in the world. If you're unhappy with Chatfuel for any reason, we provide a 30-day refund for all our paid plans. Find more details in our Terms of Use.

Where do I find my invoice?

You can find your monthly invoices under the Configure tab in your bot’s dashboard. Click on Payments, and you'll see the receipt icon next to the amount for each month.

When do I get billed?

We'll charge your credit card monthly on the same day you upgraded your plan. (If you first upgraded on the 10th, we'll charge your card every month on the 10th.) The charge will be based on your bot’s current number of reachable users for the upcoming month. We'll also add an adjustment fee at the end of the month if your user base expands to the next pricing tier within that pay period.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards including Visa, MasterCard, Discover, and American Express.

Can I cancel a paid plan any time?

Yes, you can cancel your paid plan any time. The only exception would be if we agreed beforehand on custom Chatfuel pricing with different terms.
